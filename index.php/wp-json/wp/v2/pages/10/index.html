Over the years I have participated in several conference, seminars, became editors in many of them as well. I taught in my own university as well as other universities as guest teacher in Iran, India, China and UK. I have published 2 books, attained 9 Copyrights and 3 Patents, and have several tutorials and classes online (YouTube).<\/p>
